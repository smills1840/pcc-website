Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How long does the installation take? expand_more
Most residential garage floors are completed in a single day. We arrive in the morning, and you can return to light foot traffic within a few hours. Full cure for vehicle traffic is typically 24 hours. Larger commercial projects may take 2–3 days depending on square footage.
How much does it cost? expand_more
Our full-flake polyaspartic garage coating system starts at $7.00 per square foot. A typical 2-car garage (440 sq ft) runs around $3,080. Use our instant estimator to get a ballpark for your space. Commercial projects are quoted individually — contact us for a free assessment.
Will it peel or chip? expand_more
Not if it's installed correctly. The most common reason epoxy peels is improper surface preparation. We use industrial diamond grinding on every job to open the concrete's pores and create a permanent mechanical bond — not just a surface adhesion. Our system is backed by a 20-year warranty against peeling and delamination.
Do you serve my area? expand_more
We travel up to 75 miles from the New River Valley, covering Blacksburg, Christiansburg, Radford, Roanoke, Pulaski, Dublin, Fairlawn, Wytheville, and many surrounding communities. View our full service area page or call us at (540) 212-4082 to check your location.
Is it safe for kids and pets? expand_more
Yes. Our polyaspartic topcoat is 100% VOC-free once cured. During installation we recommend keeping people and pets clear of the work area. Once the floor is cured — typically by the next morning — it is completely safe for children, pets, and vehicle traffic.
